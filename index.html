Air Pollution:



What is the problem:

Air pollution happens when particles and toxic gasses are drifting in the air. These particles and gasses can come from vehicle exhaust, factories, dust, pollen, mold spores, volcanoes, and wildfires. The particles drifting in our air are called aerosols. A lot of air pollution is created by humans, proceeding the shape of discharges from factories, cars, airplanes, or aerosol cans.

Ways we Can solve the Problem:

1:Use public transportation instead of using cars or personal vehicles
2.Turn off the lights when you are not using them
3.Recycle and Reuse
4.Don’t use plastic Bags
5.Try to reduce forest fires and Smoking
6.Try to use fans instead of using your air conditioner



Water Pollution:



What is the problem:

Water pollution happens when poisonous substances such as chemicals, trash, bacteria, and parasites enter water bodies such as lakes, rivers, oceans and so on, getting evaporated in them, lying suspended in the water or depositing on the bed.Many forms of pollution eventually make their way to oceans,rivers, and lakes. This lowers the quality of water.


Ways we Can solve the Problem:

1:If you see litter please throw it away in the trash can.
2.Blow or sweep fertilizer back onto the grass if it gets onto paved areas because if it gets on paved areas it can get to the ocean.
3.Wash your car or outdoor equipment where it can flow to a gravel or grassy area instead of a street. If it flows to the street that will go into sewage and will go into the ocean and contaminate our important supply of water.
4.Install a water-efficient toilet
5.Plastic Waste Reduction.


Global Warming:



What is the problem:

Over the past hundred years the temperatures on earth have increased 1°F even if that sounds very less, if it continues this way it will be very harmful and have many consequences. Such as Ice melts,rise in sea level,lose of coastal land,droughts, and floods.


Ways we Can solve the Problem:

1:Save energy at home
2:Walk, bike, or take public transport
3.Eat more vegetables
4.Throw away less food
5.Reduce, reuse, repair & recycle
Change your home's source of energy to Solar or Wind
6.Switch to an electric vehicle
